Entity Relationship Model for Gymnasium

Description of Relationships

Gymnasium - Member

A gymnasium can have many members, but each member is associated with only one gymnasium.

Relationship: One-to-Many (Gymnasium to Member).

Session - Member

A session can accommodate many members, and a member can register for multiple sessions.

Relationship: Many-to-Many (Session to Member).

Join Table: SessionRegistration with foreign keys SessionID and MemberID.

Session - Coach

A session can have up to two coaches, and a coach can lead multiple sessions.

Relationship: Many-to-Many (Session to Coach).

Join Table: SessionCoach with foreign keys SessionID and CoachID.
